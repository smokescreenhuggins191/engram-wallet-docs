## 🚀 Setting up your wallet

When you open the application for the first time, the software guides you through the setup.

1.  Click the icon on your desktop to launch the wallet.
2.  Select Create New Wallet.
3.  Write down your recovery seed phrase. These words act as your backup. You cannot recover your money if you lose these words.
4.  Store this paper in a place that only you can access.
5.  Create a strong password for your wallet. This password locks the software when you close it or walk away from your desk.
6.  Click Save to complete the configuration.

## 💳 How to receive payments

To receive Dero, you need to share your public address with the sender.

1.  Open the wallet.
2.  Click the Receive button.
3.  Copy your address string.
4.  Give this address to the person sending you coins.
5.  Wait for the transaction to appear in your history panel.

The wallet syncs with the network automatically. It shows your confirmed balance once the network processes the block. Do not share your recovery seed with anyone. Nobody from our team will ask for your seed phrase. Only you should possess that information.

## 📤 How to send payments

Sending coins follows a simple path.

1.  Click the Send button.
2.  Paste the recipient address in the input box.
3.  Enter the amount you wish to send.
4.  Double-check the address to avoid mistakes.
5.  Click the Confirm button.
6.  Enter your wallet password to sign the transaction.

The software sends your request to the Dero network. It deducts the amount from your wallet immediately.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be online?
Yes. The wallet contacts the Dero network to check for incoming coins and to send your transactions.

Can I move my wallet to another computer?
Yes. You use your seed phrase to restore access on any computer that runs the Engram Wallet application.

Where are my coins stored?
They sit on the Dero blockchain. Your wallet stores the keys that prove you own those coins.

What if I forget my password?
You can use your seed phrase to restore the wallet and set a new password. If you lose access to both your password and your seed phrase, you lose your coins forever.

Does this wallet support other coins?
No. This wallet specifically manages Dero assets.

How do I check the status of a transaction?
The wallet main screen shows your history. Click on any entry to see the transaction ID and current status.
